Company

We the team of Patronik Lighting Love lights and are inspired by the fact that how greater and positive difference can a good light make on our lives. Passion towards light has made Patronik Lighting today a leading Designer and Manufacturers of high-quality Architectural Lights and Automation Systems. We also undertake customized requirements of Lighting designers, Architects and Specifiers considering quality, energy efficiency, functionality and design at a time. These competences enable us to respond very flexibly to our customers’ requirements and also realise specially tailored LED-solutions in an extremely short time. Hence we tag ourselves as "Designed For You".

About Us

Company founded in year 2013 and is located in Belgaum, India. Started as an Lighting design Consultant firm, today we are leading manufactures of high-quality Architectural Lights and Automation Systems. We have our customer base all across India serving through our own research, production and sales team and a vast partner network of Developers, Contractors, Architects & Interior Designers. Patronik Lighting offers a distinctive array of solutions that reflect your individual personality, tastes and plans. We believe that the products you choose for your home should exceed functionality and transform your spaces into stunning, inspiring settings. Patronik Lighting continued pursuit of quality in everything we do is embodied in the quality of our people, our processes, our products and our services. We strive to deliver an experience of unparalleled quality to all of our internal and external constituents (customers, employees, sales representatives, vendors and end users) across all Patronik Lighting interactions. This commitment to quality will continue to be the Patronik Lighting brand’s single greatest competitive advantage and forms the foundation for future success.

Quality Associates

We are on a relentless quest for perfection and are committed to the goal of zero defects. Each product of Patronik Lighting goes through rigorous inspection and testing process. We are closely associated with ISLE(Indian society of Lighting engineers) and TUV Rheinland Group for continuous improvement of products, systems and processes. We believe that when Quality comes first reduced cost follows.
